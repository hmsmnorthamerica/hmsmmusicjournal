📋 Workflow Guide
Step 1 — Instructor: Fill in date, instrument, song list, then click Save & Notify. Parent and student get an email that the journal is ready.

Step 2 — Student: Switch to Student mode, fill in practice circles and practice time, then click Submit My Practice. Instructor gets notified.

Step 3 — Instructor: Switch back to Instructor mode, add music stamps and coins, then click Complete & Notify. Parent and student get a final summary email.
